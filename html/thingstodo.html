Brandon Hills Wildlife Management Area

A top spot for hikers, runners, skiers, and nature lovers. The Brandon Hills offer wooded trails and beautiful views, perfect for outdoor adventures in every season. The trails are made up of loops ranging from 2.5 km to 7.5 km. The Brandon Hills is located 10 kilometres south of Brandon on Highway 10, three kilometres east on Beresford Road and one kilometre south.
